## 중첩 반복문 (이중for문)

```py
for i in range(5):
    for j in range(5):
        print(j, end=" ")
    print() #줄바꿈

    print('i:', i, sep="", end=" ")
    for j in range(5):
        print('j:', j, sep="", end=" ")
```
